A project to patch Fixnum to allow conversion to roman numerals. Will be using TDD to build as an exercise.

Needs to convert
  * 1-3 to I-III

  * 4 to IV
  * 5 to V

  * 9 to IX
  * 10 to X

  * 40 to XL
  * 50 to L

  * 90 to XC
  * 100 to C

  * 400 to CD
  * 500 to D

  * 900 to CM
  * 1000 to M
